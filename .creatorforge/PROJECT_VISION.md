# CreatorForge Project Vision

## Mission

CreatorForge helps creators turn one idea into useful, high-quality content with local AI while retaining privacy, control, and low operating cost.

## Vision

Build a local-first AI content studio that turns a topic into a complete creator project: research, a concise research summary, outline, titles, script, description, tags, and thumbnail prompt.

## Long-Term Goals

- Support a full multi-platform content workflow.
- Keep local-model support through Ollama as a first-class capability.
- Provide an approachable, dependable product for individual creators and small teams.
- Build reusable Forge principles for future Phil AI Labs products.

## Product Philosophy

- Local-first where practical.
- Creator control over automation.
- Clear, editable artifacts instead of opaque one-shot output.
- Incremental delivery of end-user value.
- Maintainable engineering over unnecessary complexity.

## Target Users

- YouTube creators.
- Solo content creators.
- Small media teams.
- Privacy-conscious creators using local language models.

## Revenue Vision

CreatorForge can begin as a local productivity product and later support paid creator features such as advanced workflows, templates, export tools, team collaboration, provider integrations, and publishing automation.

## Creator Journey

1. Enter a topic and select a model.
2. Generate a research-led content project.
3. Review research, summary, outline, and delivery assets.
4. Reopen saved projects and refine content.
5. Export or publish through future creator tools.

## Future AI Ecosystem

CreatorForge is the first product in the Phil AI Labs Forge family. Future products may include CyberForge, DevForge, StudyForge, and HomeForge, sharing local-first AI, modular FastAPI backends, practical web frontends, and disciplined governance.
