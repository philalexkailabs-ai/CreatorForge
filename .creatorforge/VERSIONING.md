# Versioning

CreatorForge uses product versions for user-visible maturity and sprint identifiers for focused engineering work.

## Version Map

- **G-001, G-002:** Governance and engineering knowledge-base work.
- **0.5.x:** Foundation: project generation, model selection, history, and opening.
- **0.6.x:** AI engine: research, context-aware generation, prompt quality, progress, and distillation.
- **0.7.x:** UX and creator workflow refinement.
- **0.8.x:** Automation and release-candidate readiness.
- **1.0:** Production-ready CreatorForge release.
- **2.0:** Creator OS and broader platform capabilities.

## Rules

- Increment minor versions for meaningful user-facing capability groups.
- Use sprint identifiers for traceability in planning, changelog entries, and daily logs.
- Do not label a release production-ready until testing, deployment, security, and operational requirements are approved.

See [Roadmap](ROADMAP.md) and [Release Notes](RELEASE_NOTES.md).
