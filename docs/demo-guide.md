# 30-Second Demo Guide

## Goal

Show the complete local CreatorForge value loop in roughly 30 seconds.

## Recommended Scenes

1. **0–3 seconds:** Show the CreatorForge Studio and local-model selector.
2. **3–7 seconds:** Enter a topic and click Generate Project.
3. **7–13 seconds:** Show live progress moving through research, summary, outline, and script stages.
4. **13–21 seconds:** Show generated research, outline, titles, script, and thumbnail prompt.
5. **21–27 seconds:** Open Saved Projects and reopen the generated project.
6. **27–30 seconds:** End on the “Local AI Content Operating System” message.

## Camera Movement

Use deliberate cursor movement, short pauses, and minimal zoom. Avoid rapid pans, distracting cursor trails, or unnecessary cuts.

## GIF Optimization

- Capture at 1280 × 720 or 1440 × 810.
- Trim to the shortest useful sequence.
- Use 10–15 FPS for README GIFs.
- Compress without making text unreadable.
- Prefer MP4 for longer or higher-quality demonstrations.

## Video Optimization

- Export H.264 MP4 at 1080p when possible.
- Keep audio optional; captions should carry essential information.
- Avoid including private topics, project files, or local paths.
