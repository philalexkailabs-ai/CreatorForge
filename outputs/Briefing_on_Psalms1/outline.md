Introduction  
Section 1: Historical Context & Authorship  
Key talking points: Origin in David’s time (10th–11th century BCE), role in ancient Israelite worship, purpose as a foundational psalm for ethical guidance.  

Section 2: Literary Structure & Contrast  
Key talking points: Two-part contrast between the righteous and the wicked, symbolic tree metaphor, parallelism and imagery in poetic form.  

Section 3: Ethical Lessons & Modern Relevance  
Key talking points: Living righteously vs. sin, consequences of straying from God’s ways, parallels to contemporary ethical dilemmas (integrity, accountability).  

Section 4: Practical Applications  
Key talking points: Prioritizing spiritual nourishment, cultivating resilience through faith, integrating divine wisdom into daily decisions.  

Section 5: Common Misinterpretations to Avoid  
Key talking points: Clarifying the tree metaphor as symbolic, avoiding fragmented analysis of the psalm’s structure, recognizing its timeless applicability beyond historical context.
